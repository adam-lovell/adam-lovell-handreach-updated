Get Involved with HandReach

There are several ways to get involved with HandReach's programs, and we invite you to participate in a way that suits your schedule and interests. You can:

  • Donate: Your contributions are essential to supporting our mission, and you can make a one-time donation or join our monthly giving program by clicking on the Donate tab.
  • Volunteer: Whether you're available for a single event or a longer-term commitment, we welcome your time and skills. Learn more about volunteering by clicking on the Volunteer tab.
