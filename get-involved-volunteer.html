Volunteer with HandReach

HandReach is an all-volunteer organization, and we can only grow and flourish with the help and ideas of individuals like you who are willing to devote time and energy to the cause of helping kids recover from trauma. Whether you wish to fundraise, participate in a medical mission, or simply volunteer your time, HandReach welcomes you! Please feel free to email us at: brecken@handreach.org
